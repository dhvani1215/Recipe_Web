ü•Ñ Ingredients

  • 1 lb penne or fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions

Time Remaining: 45:00
  1. 1 Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente.

  3. 3 Add broccoli and bell peppers to skillet. Cook for 3-4 minutes until slightly tender.

  6. 6 Pour in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. 7 Drain pasta and add to the skillet with vegetables. Toss to combine.
  8. 8 Add Parmesan cheese and fresh basil. Serve immediately while hot!

ü•Ñ Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 can (14oz) coconut milk
  • 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Basmati rice for serving

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions

Time Remaining: 65:00
  1. 1 Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. 2 Brown chicken pieces on all sides, about 6-8 minutes total. Remove and set aside.
  3. 3 In the same pot, add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. 4 Add garlic, ginger, curry powder, turmeric, and cumin. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. 5 Stir in tomato paste and cook for another minute.
  6. 6 Add diced tomatoes and coconut milk. Bring to a simmer.
  7. 7 Return chicken to pot and simmer for 25-30 minutes until chicken is tender.
  8. 8 Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve over basmati rice with fresh cilantro!
